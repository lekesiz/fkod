# F-KOD: Executive Summary

## Company Overview

**F-KOD (Fıtrat Kodları)** is a global platform connecting 14-24 year olds with mentors for personalized growth, self-discovery, and career development. The platform combines a proprietary archetype discovery system, AI-powered mentor matching, and an online learning platform to help young people understand their potential and accelerate their personal development.

**Founded:** 2026  
**Headquarters:** Turkey (with regional offices in Germany, USA, India)  
**Team Size:** 12 (expanding to 30+ by Month 6)  
**Funding Sought:** $5M Series A

---

## The Problem

1.8 billion young people globally lack personalized guidance for their future. Research shows that 65% of youth feel uncertain about their career path, 72% lack access to quality mentorship, and 78% want personalized guidance. Traditional education focuses on subjects, not personal potential discovery. Quality mentorship is expensive and inaccessible to most young people.

---

## The Solution

F-KOD provides a comprehensive platform combining three core elements:

**1. Archetype Discovery Test:** A proprietary 10-question test identifying 12 unique archetypes based on psychological frameworks. Generates personalized insights and recommendations helping young people understand their strengths and potential.

**2. Intelligent Mentor Matching:** AI-powered algorithm matching mentees with mentors considering archetype, goals, availability, and expertise. 85%+ matching accuracy with real-time messaging and session scheduling.

**3. Continuous Learning Platform:** 100+ courses tailored to each archetype with video lessons, progress tracking, certificates, and gamification.
